# Thrivn — Vision Statement

> **Excel at life by finding balance.**
>
> Thrivn reveals where you're thriving and where you need attention — across your physical,
> mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being. Through daily check-ins, habit tracking, and
> an AI coach that understands your goals, it helps you align how you spend your time with
> what truly matters.

---

## Guiding Principles

All feature decisions should align with these:

1. **Balance over perfection.** The app doesn't push users to max every area. It helps them find intentional allocation that fits their current life season.

2. **Awareness first, action second.** Reveal patterns before prescribing changes. Users make better decisions when they see the data.

3. **Encouraging, never judgmental.** The tone is a supportive coach, not a drill sergeant. Celebrate progress, gently surface gaps.

4. **Simplicity in complexity.** Life is complex; the app should feel simple. Reduce friction for daily use — check-ins should take under 60 seconds.

5. **Privacy by design.** User data stays on-device (with optional iCloud sync). No third-party analytics. The AI coach sees context to help, not to harvest.

---

## Core Loop

```
Check in → Track → Reflect → Adjust → Repeat
```

1. **Check in** — 4x daily rituals (mood, energy, reflection)
2. **Track** — Tasks, habits, focus sessions across life dimensions
3. **Reflect** — Life Compass scores, pattern insights, weekly reviews
4. **Adjust** — AI coach suggests rebalancing based on real data
5. **Repeat** — Small daily actions compound into life-level change
